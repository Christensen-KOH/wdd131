Ivory Coast

Côte d'Ivoire

About

Ivory Coast, officially the Republic of Côte d'Ivoire, is a West African nation known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse wildlife, and cocoa production. The country is home to vibrant cities, beautiful beaches, and tropical rainforests.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Yamoussoukro (official), Abidjan (de facto)
  • Population: ~27 million
  • Language: French
  • Currency: West African CFA franc
  • Area: 322,463 km²

Culture

Ivory Coast has a vibrant cultural scene with traditional music, dance, and art. The country is famous for its Basidjan International Festival, traditional mask carving, and distinctive textile patterns. The people are known for their warm hospitality and love of celebration.
